Yukar

/toán pe utasa kor kamúj otta eársu pa kor…/
/népta kor?e kor an kor, kannakamúj anakné earká?e./
"Going across that place toward the deity, they walked… 'What is it that you seek?' As they went, the thunder deity was troubled."
